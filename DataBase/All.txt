Birds are animals with feathers and wings.

*   Birds lay eggs to have baby birds.
*   They can fly using their wings.
*   Birds eat seeds, insects, or fruits.
*   Many birds sing pretty songs.
*   They live in nests.

Example:

*   A blue bird flies.
*   It builds a cozy nest.
*   It lays three blue eggs.
*   Mommy bird brings food.
*   Baby birds hatch soon.
